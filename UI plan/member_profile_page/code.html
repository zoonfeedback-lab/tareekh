Dr. Elias Al-Farabi

"Preservation is not merely an act of storage, but a living dialogue between the silence of the past and the noise of the future."

Scholarly Biography

Dr. Elias Al-Farabi serves as a Senior Archivist within the Tareekh Collective, specializing in the Andalusian Golden Age and the translation of 12th-century nautical manuscripts. His tenure at the archive has been defined by a meticulous approach to digitizing fading scripts.

Before joining Tareekh, he spent a decade in Fes, Morocco, documenting the structural evolution of madrasas. His current research focuses on the intersection of astronomical mathematics and the decorative arts in late Fatimid architecture.
